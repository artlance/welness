Day 1 - Exercise 1
Duration
45 Seconds
Rest time
30 seconds
Repetitions
10
Type of exercise
Stretching
Difficulty
2

Start point:

Begin in a kneeling position, hands-on thighs and toes curled underneath the buttocks.

Action:

Move forward on the knees bringing the forearms to the floor, hands close together. Lift the legs and take the weight through the arms and toes. Bring the left knee up as if towards the shoulder, then extend the leg again.

Tips:

  • Engage the core muscles
  • Position the arms so they form the foundations of the movement.
